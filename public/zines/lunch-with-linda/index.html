Lunch with Linda

A collaborative mini zine made in Clio’s

October 25, 2024

Dillon and I made this together while sharing a few drinks in Clio’s on Grand Ave in Oakland! It was made on 65 lb cardstock with Tombow brush pens of assorted colors. It’s based on a typo that Dillon made during their freshman year of college.
